** Position of wǒ and zài

*** wǒ zài yào yī wàn fàn
- I further/still want one bowl of rice
*** nín hái yào bié de ma
- You further/still want more (dishes)?
*** nǐ hái yào hē shǔi ma

- You further/still want to drink water?
*** wǒ yào yī gè …
- I want one …
*** (wǒ) hái yào yī gè …
- Further (I) want one …
*** (wǒ) zài yào yī gè …
- Further (I) want one …
